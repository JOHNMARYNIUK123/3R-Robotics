Red River Resistance

3R Robotics

We are one of 3,000 teams participating worldwide in the FIRST ROBOTICS COMPETITION. We represent Manitoba as the only Manitoban team participating in FRC.

Our objective is for students to learn about business, engineering, programming, project management, leadership, and creative skills with skilled mentors in an exciting, competitive environment.

We just finished our rookie season of FRC and it was a huge success! We competed at the Great Nothern Regional in Grand Forks MN, placing 20th of 56 teams and winning the Rookie Inspiration Award.
